# Privacy Policy for PDF Pocket

**Last updated: March 16, 2026**

---

## Introduction

PDF Pocket ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

**By using PDF Pocket, you agree to the terms described in this policy.**

---

## Information We Collect

**PDF Pocket does not collect or store any personal information.**

### Data Processing

All PDF processing operations (merge, split, compress, OCR, encryption, page management, etc.) are performed **locally on your device**. Your files never leave your device, except when you explicitly use cloud-based features (see below).

### Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

#### Local Processing (No Data Transmission)
- **Google ML Kit Text Recognition**: Used for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) feature. All text recognition is performed on-device. No data is sent to Google servers.
- **Apache PDFBox**: Used for PDF processing operations. All processing is done locally.

#### Cloud Processing (Optional Features)
- **Google Gemini AI**: When you use the **AI Summary** feature, the text content of your PDF is sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. This is the **only feature** that transmits data off your device. Google may process this data according to their [Gemini API Terms](https://ai.google.dev/terms). We do not store or share this data beyond what is necessary for the feature to work.

### Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Storage Access**: To read PDF files and images you select, and to save processed files to your device.
- **Microphone Access** (optional): Only used for the **Voice to PDF** feature. Audio is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted or stored.

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission (except for AI Summary as noted above).

---

## Data Storage

- All processed files are saved to your device's local storage (Documents/PDFPocket folder).
- The app stores minimal preferences locally (language settings, theme, trial usage counts) using Android SharedPreferences.
- **No data is uploaded to any server or cloud service**, except when you explicitly use the AI Summary feature.

---

## In-App Purchases

The app offers the following purchase options to unlock Pro features:

- Monthly subscription ($2.99/month)
- Annual subscription ($14.99/year)
- Lifetime license (one-time purchase)

Purchase information is handled by Google Play Billing and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, etc.).

---

## How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Core Features**: Process PDFs locally on your device (merge, split, compress, OCR, etc.).
- **AI-Powered Features**: Send PDF text to Gemini API only when you explicitly use the AI Summary feature.
- **Subscription Management**: Verify your Pro subscription status via Google Play Billing.

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties.

---

## Children's Privacy

PDF Pocket is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete it.

## Your Rights and Choices

- **Access and Delete**: Since all files are stored locally on your device, you can access or delete them at any time through your device's file manager or within the app.
- **Uninstall**: Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data from your device.
