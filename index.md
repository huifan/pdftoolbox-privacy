# Privacy Policy for PDF Toolbox

**Last updated: March 15, 2026**

## Introduction

PDF Toolbox ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**PDF Toolbox does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

### Data Processing

All PDF processing operations (merge, split, compress, OCR, encryption, etc.) are performed **locally on your device**. Your files never leave your device.

### Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that process data locally on your device:

- **Google ML Kit Text Recognition**: Used for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) feature. All text recognition is performed on-device. No data is sent to Google servers.
- **Apache PDFBox**: Used for PDF processing operations. All processing is done locally.

### Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

- **Storage Access**: To read PDF files and images you select, and to save processed files to your device.

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and do not involve data collection or transmission.

## Data Storage

- All processed files are saved to your device's local storage (Documents/PDFToolbox folder).
- The app stores minimal preferences locally (language settings, trial usage counts) using Android SharedPreferences.
- No data is uploaded to any server or cloud service.

## In-App Purchases

The app offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock Pro features. Purchase information is handled by Google Play Billing and is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Children's Privacy

PDF Toolbox does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
